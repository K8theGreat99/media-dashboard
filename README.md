# Media Dashboard
## Break Free from the Algorithm

Media Dashboard gives you an alternative to drinking from the algorithmic firehouse: sipping from your own curated list of books, blogs, and YouTube channels. It presents the most recent posts from your favorite sources, allows you to mark them as "read/watched" so they disappear from your custom feed, and limits your feed instead of constantly bombarding you with new things to try. 

It's not meant to be a total replacement for the "for you" feed, but a daily driver that makes sure you never miss your favorite stuff without assaulting you with a never-ending onslaught of content.

Stay focused without missing your favorite content.
